About Us

Meatvo is a hyperlocal raw meat delivery app serving Bokaro and surrounding areas in Jharkhand. We connect you with fresh, quality chicken, mutton, and fish — cut and packed to order — delivered straight to your doorstep.

Our mission is to make buying fresh meat as easy as ordering anything else online: browse, checkout, and receive your order within the hour.

Services & Pricing

Prices are shown in the app and updated regularly to reflect current market rates. All product listings include weight, cut type, and per-unit pricing before you add to cart.

We offer fresh chicken, mutton, and fish delivered to your door. Select your items, choose a delivery slot, and pay via Cash on Delivery or online payment at checkout.

Final prices displayed at checkout are inclusive of applicable charges. Download the Meatvo app to browse the full catalogue and place your order.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Order Cancellation by Customer

You may cancel an order free of charge before it enters the "Preparing" stage. Once preparation has begun, cancellation may not be possible as fresh products are cut and packed to order. To cancel, use the in-app cancel option or contact support@meatvo.in immediately.

Cancellation by Meatvo

We reserve the right to cancel an order if a product is unavailable, your address is outside our service area, payment cannot be verified, or we suspect fraudulent activity. You will receive a full refund for any prepaid amount in such cases.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued in the following situations:

  • Order cancelled by Meatvo due to unavailability or service issues
  • Wrong item delivered — report within 2 hours of delivery with photo evidence
  • Product quality issue (spoiled, incorrect weight, or not fresh) — report within 2 hours of delivery
  • Order not delivered within the promised window due to our fault

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after order preparation has started
  • Incorrect address or contact details provided by the customer
  • Customer unavailable at the delivery location after multiple attempts
  • Products stored improperly after delivery

Refund Processing

Approved refunds for online payments are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. COD orders eligible for refund will be credited via UPI or bank transfer after verification. Contact support@meatvo.in to initiate a refund request.

Meatvo currently delivers to selected localities in and around Bokaro, Jharkhand. Enter your address in the app to check whether your location is serviceable before placing an order.

Express 1-Hour Delivery

Our express delivery promise aims to deliver your order within 1 hour of confirmation, subject to product availability, order volume, and weather or traffic conditions. Delivery times shown in the app are estimates and not guaranteed in exceptional circumstances.

Delivery Slots

You may choose an available delivery slot at checkout. We pack orders fresh to your selected slot. Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address during the chosen time window.

Packaging

All meat products are hygienically packed in sealed, food-grade packaging to maintain freshness and temperature during transit. We recommend refrigerating products immediately upon receipt.

Delivery Charges

Applicable delivery fees, if any, are displayed at checkout before you confirm your order. Promotional free delivery offers, when available, will be reflected in the app.
